Common Questions ​

Q: What sort of custom development do you provide? ​

On the technology side of things, we specialise in: AWS or GCP cloud management, CI/CD pipelines, WordPress, CouchDB/MongoDB, Postgres and MariaDB(MySQL). We have worked on Ruby (Sinatra), Python and PHP based projects on various scales and sizes.

On the platform infrastructure side, we provide consultancy, upgrades and training on docker, build-pipes design, CI/CD designs and kubernetes. We operate remotely. Send us an email for a quick chat to discuss.

Q: I need a basic site, in the static pages or wordpress? ​

The pages are limited to a setup of 5 pages on wordpress. We include a free walk-through during consultation on how to setup new pages and at the moment, however there is a soft limit of 5 pages (with flexible options for more). The sites are hosted on our platform wordpress project site https://sites.alldaycity.com.

Q: What does your team do for software consultancy? ​

We consult for web based technology, primarily cloud infrastructures. We also consult for various web development projects, cloud resource management, project management and security. Our fixed pricing and quotes are fair; as we aim to provide a “surprises-free” partnership.
